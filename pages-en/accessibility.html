Accessibility Statement

The Republic of Insulide is committed to ensuring this website is accessible to all persons, regardless of ability or the technology used to access it.

Our Commitment

The Government of Insulide aims to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards across all official digital properties. We believe access to government information is a right, not a privilege.

This website has been designed with the following considerations:

  • Sufficient colour contrast across all text and interactive elements
  • Keyboard navigability throughout all pages
  • Descriptive link text and semantic HTML structure
  • Scalable text that responds to browser zoom settings
  • All three official languages — English, French, and Russian — available from every page

Known Limitations

We are aware that some areas of this site are still being improved. Current known limitations include:

  • Some decorative images may not yet have full alternative text descriptions
  • The investor enquiry form is undergoing accessibility review
  • PDF documents linked from this portal are being progressively updated to be screen-reader compatible

We are actively working to resolve these issues. If you encounter a barrier, please contact us.

Contact & Feedback

If you experience difficulty accessing any content on this website, or wish to request information in an alternative format, please contact the Office of Digital Government Services:

Email: access@gov.isl

Response time: Within 5 working days

Languages: Enquiries accepted in English, French, and Russian

Enforcement

This statement was prepared on 1 January 2024. It was last reviewed on 1 January 2025. The Government of Insulide reviews this statement annually and following any significant update to this website.
